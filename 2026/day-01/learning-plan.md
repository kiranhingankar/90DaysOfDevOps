# 🚀 90-Day DevOps Learning Plan

## 👤 Current Level

Working professional (4 years Software Engineering experience)
Basic understanding of: Shell Scripting, AWS, Docker, Jenkins, Ansible, Terraform, Kubernetes, Helm
Goal: Transition into a DevOps Engineer role with real-world project experience

---

## 🧠 My Understanding

**DevOps** is a culture and set of practices that combines development and operations to deliver software faster, more reliably, and with automation.

**Cloud Engineering** involves designing, deploying, and managing scalable infrastructure using cloud platforms like AWS.

---

## 🎯 Why I Am Learning DevOps & Cloud

* To transition from a traditional development role to a high-demand DevOps role
* To work on scalable systems and automation-driven infrastructure
* To increase career growth, salary potential, and real-world impact

---

## 🏁 Where I Want to Reach (90 Days Goal)

1. Build and deploy a **production-grade CI/CD pipeline** using Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes
2. Provision complete infrastructure using **Terraform + AWS** (IaC approach)
3. Deploy and manage a microservices-based app on **Kubernetes with monitoring & logging**

---

## 🛠️ Core Skills to Build

1. **CI/CD Pipelines & Automation**

2. **Cloud & Infrastructure as Code**

   * AWS services (EC2, S3, IAM, VPC), Terraform

3. **Containerization & Orchestration**

   * Docker, Kubernetes (deployment, debugging, scaling)
